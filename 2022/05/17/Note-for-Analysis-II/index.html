Note for Analysis II

This is the note for the course Analysis taught by Professor Yi Cheng from University of Science and Technology of China. The noter took the course through recorded video informally. Both the teaching language and the note language is Chinese.

The course focused on developing student skills in proving and understanding mathematical concepts. The course goes from Analytic Geometry and then moved on to discuss the Multivariable Calculus. Then, the concepts of curvilinear integrals and surface integrals are discussed. After that, the theme changed to Fourier analysis. Finally, the Gamma function and Beta function are introduced.

In the course, Professor Chen tells the whole story in a clear way. Due to the limitation of time, some proofs are not given. The professor sometimes likes to use examples from physics, which makes the questions become very clear and easy to understand.
