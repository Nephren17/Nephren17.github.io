About Me

I am an undergraduate in Computer Science and Engineering Department at the University of Michigan (Umich). Before my transfer, I also studied at Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU) and majored in Electrical and Computer Engineering.

My research interest lies in nonlinear control, cell manipulation and medical robotics. We hope to utilize precise control of medical robots to conduct cell manipulation, especially for oocyte genetic testing. Please feel free to reach me out if there is opportunity for possible research collaboration.
