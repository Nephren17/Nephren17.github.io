Note for Advanced Algebra

This is the note for the course Advanced Algebra taught by Professor WeiSheng Qiu from Peking University. The noter took the course through recorded video informally. Both the teaching language and the note language is Chinese.

The course started with solving Linear equations. Then, it continues to talk about linear space, including subspace, rank, and other concepts. After that, it covers the calculation of matrices. Then, it begins to talk about another Algebra area about polynomial and rings. After introducing that, the course goes back to linear mapping. After that, bilinear function and normal vector space are introduced. Finally, the quadratic form is covered.

Professor Qiu was very passionate and knowledgeable. During the lectures, he was always trying to tell students more about the mathematical world. His lectures contain many things related to basic knowledge, and he always explained how all the different concepts in algebra are connected with each other. His course developed algebra in a very compact way and introduced the wide applications of algebra.

The cover of the note is Shiina Mashiro from The Pet Girl of Sakurasou. The reason for choosing her picture to be the cover is that she is a genius, and the noter believes only a genius can fully understand this course. The noter finds it extremely hard to understand the relationships between space and the concept of the ring. Besides, the first page of the note is the lyric of Kimi ga Yume wo Tsuretekita (君が夢を連れてきた), the theme song of The Pet Girl of Sakurasou.
