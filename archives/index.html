About Me

I am an undergraduate in Data Science at the University of Michigan (Umich). Before transferring to Umich, I also studied at Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU) and majored in Electrical and Computer Engineering.

My research interest mainly lies in AI4Sci (applying AI to scientific problems). Currently, I am interested in the extrapolation of ignition delay for unknown fuels from known fuels using neural networks. I hope to find a way to roughly understand the combustion properties of certain unknown fuels before experimenting.

I am applying for Ph.D. programs and expect to graduate in the Summer of 2025. Please reach out to me if you are looking for Ph.D. students.
