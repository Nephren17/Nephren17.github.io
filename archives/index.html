About Me

I am an undergraduate in Data Science at the University of Michigan (Umich). Before transferring to Umich, I also studied at Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU) and majored in Electrical and Computer Engineering.

My research interest lies in robot control, cell manipulation and medical robotics. We hope to utilize precise control of medical robots to conduct cell manipulation, especially for oocyte genetic testing. Please feel free to reach me out if there is opportunity for possible research collaboration.

Outside academia, my interests include watching animations, reading novels, and traveling. One of my favorite novelists is Izumi Kyoka, and Three Days of Happiness is one of my favorite novel. For animations, I watched Suka Suka 6 times for its fantastic scenario.
